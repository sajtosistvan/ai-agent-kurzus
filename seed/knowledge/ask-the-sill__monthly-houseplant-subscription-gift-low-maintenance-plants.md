# A Monthly Plant Subscription to Gift This Holiday

Ask The Sill



I need a great gift for my sister. She loves plants, but is hesitant to have them as her own. What’s a good choice for her?” - Becky, Boston

###### From flawlessly introducing a trendy plant to tackling windowless room woes, our plant specialist Chrissy will set you up with the perfect plant pick.

### I need a great gift for my sister. She loves plants, but is hesitant to have them as her own. What’s a good choice for her?” - Becky, Boston

Our monthly subscription for beginners is a great option to send over to someone that is a plant lover, but hasn’t introduced any to their space just yet. Rather than getting all of their new plants at once — they’ll get a new one every month to add to their growing collection (in an easy to handle way).

You can choose between either a three month or six month plan (depending on how much space they have and your budget!) Once you order this subscription, your sister will receive an email letting her know that you just gifted her something pretty awesome.

From there she can update her delivery address, the color of the planters that she would prefer, and when she’d like to start receiving her new plants! Every month she’ll receive a new variety potted up and delivered right to her door. Happy gifting!
