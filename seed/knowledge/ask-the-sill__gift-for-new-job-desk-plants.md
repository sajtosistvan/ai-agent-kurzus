# The Best Gift to Say Congratulations on a New Job

Ask The Sill



“My friend just started a new job. I’d love to give her a plant that says good luck and send positive vibes, what do you recommend?”— Amy, Denver

###### From flawlessly introducing a trendy plant to tackling windowless room woes—plant enthusiast and customer happiness team lead Chrissy will set you up with the perfect plant pick.

### “My friend just started a new job. I’d love to give her a plant that says good luck and send positive vibes, what do you recommend?” – Amy, Denver

Hi Amy! I know a plant that would be perfect for this—the Money Tree is said to bring good luck and prosperity, making it the ideal addition to any new workspace.

This plant is commonly used in feng shui as it creates positive energy and will certainly help bring only the best of vibes to a new job. Whether it’s kept in their office or their home, we’re sure this plant will boost their confidence—and hopefully their bank account—(even if they don’t believe in these legends!)

With its signature braided trunk and plentiful leaves, this popular choice will grow to be even more eye-catching with time. Being kept in some bright indirect light and watered when soil is dry is all this lucky gift needs to thrive.
