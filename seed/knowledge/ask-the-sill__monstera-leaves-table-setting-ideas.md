### “I’m hosting a dinner party and am looking for unique table decoration ideas. I want to make my table setting pop with greenery. Any thoughts?” Jenny, New York City

Hosting a dinner party is both a fun and tough task at hand. While nailing down the menu is one thing, it’s the decorations that are truly the tricky part.

Skip generic tablescape ideas and take yours to the next level with the help of some trendy greenery. What do you need? Cut Monstera leaves.

Aside from the fact that these will last longer than flowers (you’ll get to reap the benefits long after your friends head home), these are also guaranteed to be the highlight of your swanky setup. Arrange these on your table for some Insta-worthy appeal in the most minimalist of ways.

Since these are cut fresh and shipped directly from Hawaii, this dozen of 8-12” leaves will last a few weeks in your home. Only have low light (or none at all)? No worries! Since these are already cut, they don’t need any special care.
