# A Unique Housewarming Gift

Ask The Sill



“My daughter is moving out and I want to send a housewarming gift her way. Do you have any cute gift sets that will be perfect for this?” Liz, Washington D.C.

###### From flawlessly introducing a trendy plant to tackling windowless room woes, our plant specialist Chrissy will set you up with the perfect plant pick.

### “My daughter is moving out and I want to send a housewarming gift her way. Do you have any cute gift sets that will be perfect for this?” Liz, Washington D.C.

Hi Liz! Absolutely. A great option for this is our assorted air plants paired with the Tillandz stands. Aside from their stylish appeal, these are also easy to care for if your daughter is busy unpacking and setting up her new place.

While these epiphytes are popular on their own, the added accessories to prop them up will take them from an ordinary to a super memorable gift. With some sharp modern edge, these structural standouts will definitely pop anywhere they’re kept. From situated on a shelf to being hung up on the wall, this setup will make her think of mom every time she seems ‘em.

With a quick misting daily and a good soak once a week, these unique plants are a no-fail gift option for just about any level plant owner. She lives with some curious furry companions? No worries since these are pet-friendly!
